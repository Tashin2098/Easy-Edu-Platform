For Classes 1 to 3

Home

Mathematics

Numbers and Counting:

Learning to count forwards and backwards.

Basic Arithmetic:

Understanding basic arithmetic symbols (+, -, ×, ÷).

Science

Living and Non-Living Things:

Understanding basic needs of living organisms (food, water, air, shelter).

Earth and Space:

Basic understanding of the solar system (sun, moon, stars).

Environmental Awareness:

Learning about recycling and reducing waste.

English

Speaking and Listening:

Listening to stories, poems, and informational texts read aloud.

Grammar and Punctuation:

Learning basic grammar concepts such as nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs.

Reading Comprehension:

Making predictions and inferences based on text.

History and Geography

Basic Geography:

Identifying major geographical features such as mountains, rivers, and deserts.

Community and Global Connections:

Learning about basic concepts related to trade, transportation, and communication.
