For Classes 11 to 12

Home

Mathematics

Trigonometry:

Introducing trigonometric functions (sine, cosine, tangent) and their applications in right triangles.

Coordinate Geometry:

Learn about the equations of lines, distance between points, slope of a line, equations of circles, and their properties.

Vector Algebra:

Understand vector representation, addition, subtraction, scalar and vector multiplication, and properties of vectors.

Physics

Kinematics:

Study motion in one and two dimensions, including concepts of displacement, velocity, acceleration, and equations of motion.

Electromagnetism:

Explore electric current, resistance, Ohm's law, Kirchhoff's laws, and series and parallel circuits.

Modern Physics:

Introducing the structure of the atom, subatomic particles, and nuclear reactions.

Chemistry

Inorganic Chemistry:

Learn about coordination complexes, ligands, coordination numbers, and Werner's theory.

Organic Chemistry:

Understand the classification, nomenclature, isomerism, reactions, and properties of hydrocarbons, including alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, and aromatic compounds.

Industrial Chemistry:

Explore the importance of catalysts in industrial processes, types of catalysts, and mechanisms of catalytic reactions.

Biology

Cell Biology:

Learning about cellular processes such as cell division (mitosis and meiosis), cellular respiration, photosynthesis, and protein synthesis.

Genetic Engineering:

Learning about the structure and function of DNA and RNA molecules, including DNA replication, transcription, and translation.

Microbiology:

Explore the diversity of microorganisms, including bacteria, archaea, fungi, protists, and viruses, and their roles in ecosystems, human health, and biotechnology.
